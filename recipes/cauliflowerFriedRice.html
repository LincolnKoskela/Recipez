Recipe

Description

Cauliflower Fried Rice is the perfect keto friendly dish that brings back old memories to when you used to eat however many carbs you want at your favorite restaurant or as your go to meals.

I know I always used to make chicken fried rice just about everyday. Well now we put a spin on it to fit your ketogenic goals. Look no further than Cauliflower Fried Rice!

Ingredients

  • Cauliflower
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Shallots
  • Eggs
  • Carrots & Peas
  • Scallions

How to Make

  1. Cut or break the cauliflower into more or less evenly sized florets. Then place the cauliflower into a processor just until about rice sized. Do it in batches to get a more uniform size. Try to avoid overprocessing as it may lose it texture. Transfer shredded cauliflower into a towel to squeeze out any excess liquid over the sink. Set aside.
  2. Quickly stir the soy sauce, mirin, and sugar together in small bowl until the sugar is desolved. Set aside.
  3. Slice shallots thinly and evenly. Grab a skillet, add oil, and heat over medium high until shimmering. Skillet and oil must be hot before adding the shallots. Add shallots and stir occasionally, until golden brown. About less than 3 minutes. Overcooking is better than undercooking. Once golden brown, transfer the shallots to a paper towel to drain. Keep 1 tsbp of oil left and discard the rest from the skillet.
  4. Scrammble the eggs and place onto a plate
  5. Fry the veggies
  6. Finally add the cauliflower rice into the mix with the veggies in the skillet and cook stirring occasionally, until slightly softened, about 2 minutes. Now crank up that heat to high and add the soy sauce mixture. Cook and stir often until mostly evaporated. About 1 minute. Bring the eggs back into the skillet, break up evenly, stir, and distribute. Season the skillet with salt and peper.
  7. Finish things off by sprinkling sliced scallions, fried shallots, and sesame seeds over.
  8. DONE
