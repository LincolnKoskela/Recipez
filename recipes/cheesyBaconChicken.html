Recipe

Description

Bacon and ranch combination is bliss. Combining that with a pleasant chicken dinner will make this dish your weeknight goto.

With 30 minutes, this dish will come out to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Bacon
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ranch Seasoning
  • Chives
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook bacon, flipping once, until crispy, about 8 minutes. Drain bacon fat from the skillet.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Return skillet to medium-high heat, add chicken and cook until golden and just through about 6 minutes per side.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and sprinkle chicken with ranch seasoning and top with Mozzarella cheese. Cover the skillet and cook until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 5 minutes.
  4. Crumble and sprinkle bacon and chives on top before serving
